Background

LCFCD is an evolution from Kibera Community Youth Program (KCYP) starting way back in 2013 through the efforts of a number of like-minded people with diverse professional backgrounds and experience, with a view of giving back to the community through different aspects of empowerment.

The idea was born as a result of constant interaction with the community and realizing the different need gaps as well optimizing on the opportunities therein.

The constitution was then redrafted, discussed and agreed upon and now serves as a pillar for our operations towards the set goals.
